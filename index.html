Respect & Conduct

We will not tolerate any derogatory comments regarding race, sexual preference, disabilities, age, or gender. We strive for this to be a safe place where people can go to have fun. Any use of this type of language will result in disciplinary action. RP that involves torture, suicide, terrorism, or sexual assault is strictly prohibited.

  • Listen to staff instructions; disobeying staff may result in warnings or bans.
  • Be patient with new players and help maintain a positive community.

Personal Messaging

No Personal Messaging Outside of RP:

  • Do not message other players personally on Discord. All interactions must remain in RP context.
  • You may use general chat for out-of-character discussions, but personal messaging outside the server is prohibited.
  • If you have any concerns with another member, bring it to a moderator or admin.
  • Violating this rule may result in a warning or further disciplinary action.
  • Let's keep our community fun and respectful!

Roleplay Rules

  • Stay in Character (IC) at all times. Out of Character (OOC) chat should only occur in designated areas.
  • No Power-gaming: Do not force actions on other players without consent.
  • No Meta-gaming: Do not use knowledge from outside the game to influence in-game actions.
  • No Fail RP: Avoid unrealistic actions that break immersion.
  • Respect realistic scenarios, medical procedures, and city life.

Combat & PvP

  • No RDM (Random Death Match) — killing without RP context is strictly prohibited.
  • No VDM (Vehicle Death Match) — using vehicles to kill outside RP context is forbidden.
  • Value of Life: All players have rights during confrontations; follow in-game surrender, negotiation, and group rules.
  • Group combat: If outnumbered, compliance is expected; no unrealistic advantage usage.
  • Use of firearms, explosives, or melee must be realistic and justified within RP context.

Criminal Activity & Robberies

  • Robberies must be properly planned and roleplayed.
  • Maximum participants and vehicles: 1–5 players depending on scenario.
  • Law enforcement must have a fair chance to respond. Wait 5 minutes after starting a robbery before leaving.
  • Excessive or unrealistic criminal behavior is prohibited.
  • Cooldowns between robberies: Minimum 30 minutes for each type of job.
  • Hostage rules: No harming hostages, realistic demands, max 3 hostages per scenario.

Police & EMS

  • Follow all instructions from PD/EMS during RP interactions.
  • You may only engage law enforcement according to server laws.
  • EMS cannot be killed; kidnapping is allowed if at least 3 EMS members are online.
  • Traffic stops and arrests must be roleplayed realistically; ignoring realistic outcomes is Fail RP.
  • PD may use reasonable force but must stay within RP context.

Green Zones

  • The only green zone in the city is Pillbox Hospital.
  • No killing, harassment, or chaos is allowed in this zone.
  • Players may use Green Zones to safely heal or plan RP activities.

Vehicles & Property

  • Speed limits: In-city 80 MPH, Highway 120 MPH.
  • No armored or weaponized vehicles unless authorized by server events.
  • Respect other players’ properties and businesses.
  • Stealing, griefing, or unrealistic vehicle abuse is prohibited.
  • Parking, traffic, and collisions must be handled realistically.

Jobs & Economy

  • Players may hold up to 2 jobs depending on server rules.
  • Whitelisted jobs (PD/EMS) have priority and restrictions on other occupations.
  • Businesses must operate fairly, with realistic RP management.
  • Business ownership requires activity checks, hiring staff, and participation in RP.
  • Unauthorized business conduct, cheating, or unrealistic profits are prohibited.

Communication & Discord

  • English is required for all in-character communications.
  • Working microphone is mandatory; use push-to-talk.
  • External communications cannot be used to gain RP advantages.
  • Follow Discord server rules in addition to in-game rules.
  • Do not leak OOC conflicts or personal information.

Recordings & Evidence

  • Players are encouraged to record RP sessions for evidence in disputes.
  • Staff may request recordings in case of conflicts.
  • Evidence will be used to make fair decisions in RP incidents.
  • Do not tamper with recordings or fabricate evidence.

Server Etiquette

  • Be polite and patient with other players and staff.
  • No spamming, trolling, or disruptive behavior.
  • Follow all rules in both Discord and in-game for a positive RP experience.
  • Encourage fair play and help new players learn RP rules.
